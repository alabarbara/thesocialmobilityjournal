About Us:

The Social Mobility Journal is a nonprofit publication that empowers youth journalism to document the lived reality of American working-class families. Through national writing competitions and workshops, SMJ offers a platform for silenced voices to be uplifted. 100% authentically human stories, unforgettable resilience.

Our Goal:

To expand nationally with paid competitions and writing workshops at 500 educational institutions by 2030.
