GUIDELINES:

1. Make a copy of the submission template.
2. Input requested personal information, the prompt for the application, and the essay draft.
3. Choose whether you want 1 round of edits or 2 rounds.
4. Within 2-3 weeks of submission, the writer will receive an email with edit suggestions. For those choosing 1 round of edits, this edited version will be published on SMJ with a title and thumbnail.
5. For those who chose 2 rounds of edits, the writer will resubmit the edited version and receive a second round of suggestions within 2-3 weeks. This edited version will be published on SMJ.



DISCLAIMER:

It is SMJ's highest priority to protect the privacy of our writers, thus, writers may choose to remain anonymous (under a pseudonym or first name) or publish their work with their real name. No identifying information beyond the student's name will be published. Writers may request to withdraw their work from SMJ at any point by submitting the Request for Entry Withdrawl Form.
